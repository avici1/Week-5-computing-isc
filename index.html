1. Assessment

Formative Assessment

Formative assessment will be by means of weekly/biweekly observation of performance and engagement in the format of class contribution, online engagement and quizzes. This provides additional regular detailed feedback to students so that they can build an awareness of their rate and level of progress and of their strengths and weaknesses. Feedback and model answers will be provided accordingly. It is expected that you always complete all the tasks but please speak to your module tutor if you have trouble keeping up

2. Feedback

Duration

All assessments and coursework will be marked within two weeks. This may be slightly longer between terms due to holidays. Coursework will be returned to you during class time so you can see what you did well and what could be improved on. For some assessment, you will be able to see the scripts, but for others, you will be provided with feedback to help you understand any errors.

Record kepping

You cannot keep the work as it has to be moderated (i.e. checked over by someone else to ensure the marking is accurate). Each module has different methods of assessment (outlined in the grid in section 8 above).

Progress monitoring
